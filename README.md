# 🇵🇰 SkillBridge AI — Pakistan's Next-Gen Skilled Work & Freelance Ecosystem

> **Empowering 100 Million+ Skilled Workers, Technicians, Freelancers & Businesses Across Pakistan with AI, Escrow Protection, and Voice Boli Intelligence.**

---

## 🚀 The Vision & Motivation

**SkillBridge AI** is designed to revolutionize how skilled professionals (solar technicians, electricians, AC HVAC mechanics, tailors, plumbers, software developers, and graphic designers) connect with clients across major Pakistani cities like Lahore, Karachi, Islamabad, Rawalpindi, Peshawar, Faisalabad, and Multan.

In traditional local marketplaces, workers face payment delays, lack of formal skill verification, and communication barriers. Clients face fraud, unverified quality, and price inflation.

**SkillBridge AI solves this with 100% server-backed AI intelligence, zero-fraud escrow protection, and multimodal voice technology.**

---

## 🔥 Key Powerful Features

### 🎙️ 1. Awaaz Boli (Voice-to-Job Post)
- **Roman Urdu & English Voice Parsing**: Speak requirements in everyday Roman Urdu or English (e.g., *"Gulberg Lahore me Growatt inverter wiring aur net metering chahiye"*).
- **Gemini AI Extraction**: Automatically parses city, neighborhood, budget in PKR, category, required skills, and urgency.

### 🛡️ 2. Pakistan AI Escrow Vault
- **0% Fraud Guarantee**: Funds are securely locked in JazzCash, EasyPaisa, SadaPay, NayaPay, or 1IBFT Bank accounts before work begins.
- **Milestone Payouts**: Payouts are released only after client satisfaction and AI work completion verification.

### 📸 3. AI Work Quality Inspector (Gemini Multimodal Vision)
- **Visual Proof Audit**: Workers upload completion photos (e.g. solar DB wiring, AC gas pressure brazing, tailored sherwani stitching).
- **Quality & Safety Rating**: AI analyzes neatness, insulation, grounding, and awards a **Verified Quality Certificate** with score (0-100%).

### 🚨 4. AI Anti-Scam & Fraud Scanner
- **Real-time Chat Security**: Scans messaging threads for off-platform advance payment demands, phishing, or scam keywords.
- **Instant Risk Score**: Displays a clear warning banner with safety guidelines in Roman Urdu.

### 🎙️ 5. AI Live Oral Technical Interview Room
- **Interactive Quiz Simulator**: Workers answer 2 technical & customer handling oral questions in Roman Urdu or English.
- **Instant Verification Badge**: Evaluates technical competence and grants **AI Gold Certified Master Specialist** status on worker profiles.

### 📊 6. Pak-Rate 2026 Price Benchmark Calculator
- **Fair Pricing Engine**: Calculates realistic market rates for labor and materials across Pakistani cities to eliminate overcharging and underbidding.

## 🛠️ Backend Architecture & APIs

The backend is built with **Node.js, Express, and Google Gemini 3.6 Flash AI**:

1. `POST /api/ai/voice-to-job` — Transcribes Roman Urdu voice scripts into structured JSON job postings.
2. `POST /api/ai/inspect-work` — Multimodal vision analysis of completed work proof photos.
3. `POST /api/escrow/deposit` & `/api/escrow/release` — Secure server transaction ledger for JazzCash/EasyPaisa escrow holding.
4. `POST /api/ai/fraud-scan` — Intelligent chat threat detection and scam prevention.
5. `POST /api/ai/live-interview` — Automated oral technical interview evaluation & badge certification.
6. `POST /api/ai/price-calculator` — City-specific labor and material rate benchmark engine.
7. `POST /api/ai/chat-assistant` — Conversational assistant supporting Roman Urdu, English, and technical job matching.
